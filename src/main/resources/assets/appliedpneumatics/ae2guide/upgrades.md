## Volume Card
<RecipeFor id="volume_card" />  
Can be used in the **ME Pressure Interface** and the **ME Temperature Interface** to increase base volume or base heat capacity.  
- Higher volume: stores more air at the same pressure.  
- Higher heat capacity: stores more heat at the same temperature.  

## Security Card
<RecipeFor id="security_card" />  
Can be used in the **ME Pressure Interface** and **Air Storage Cells**.  
- In the Pressure Interface: prevents explosions caused by excessive pressure.  
- In Air Storage Cells: prevents overfilling that would eject the cell from the drive or even cause the drive to explode.  

## Vacuum Card
<RecipeFor id="vacuum_card" />  
Can be used in the **ME Pressure Interface** and **Air Storage Cells**.  
- In the Pressure Interface: allows the target pressure to be set negative, replacing the function of a vacuum pump.  
- In Air Storage Cells: destroys excess air when overfilled.  

## Charging Card
<RecipeFor id="charging_card" />  
Can be used in **Portable Air Storage Cells**, allowing them to automatically charge air-using items in the player’s inventory.
