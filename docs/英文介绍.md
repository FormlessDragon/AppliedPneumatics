# Applied Pneumatics

![banner](https://i.imgur.com/ZUmdWWw.png)

> **An AE2 × PneumaticCraft: Repressurized add-on**
> Brings **compressed air** natively into the ME network and integrates **Amadron** trading into AE automation.

---

## Feature Overview

* **Buses fully recognize air**: Import/Export/Storage Buses can charge/discharge and store air for blocks that provide an air capability.
* **P2P: Air & Temperature**: Two P2P tunnel types for **high-speed transfer** of air and temperature inside the ME network; tune with the corresponding items via right-click (see in-game GuideME for details).
* **ME Pressure Interface**: Pressure regulation; can **pull air from the world under vacuum** (with a Vacuum Card).
* **ME Temperature Interface**: Temperature regulation; uses **AE power + compressed air** for fast heating/cooling.
* **Air Storage Cells (incl. Portable)**: Capacity tiers from 1k up to 256m; **portable cells** also work as high-capacity air tanks (**up to 20 bar**).
* **Amadron Wireless Terminal + Processing Station**: The terminal can place orders using ME resources; **unlimited** trades can be made into **patterns** and participate in AE autocrafting.
* **Upgrade Cards**: Safety Card, Vacuum Card, Volume Card, Charging Card.

---

## Main Blocks & Items

### P2P Tunnels (Air / Temperature)

* Transfer the corresponding medium inside the ME network; **right-click with the specified items** to tune/bind.
* For more details and diagrams, see **GuideME** in-game.

### ME Pressure Interface

* Set a **target pressure**; automatically takes/stores air to/from the ME network to keep it stable; can connect directly to PNC pipes; includes an item slot to charge/drain canisters.
* Supported Upgrades: **Volume Card, Vacuum Card, Safety Card**.

### ME Temperature Interface

* Set a **target temperature**; consumes AE power and air to heat/cool; suitable for both cooling and heating workflows.
* Supported Upgrades: **Acceleration Card, Volume (Thermal Capacity) Card**.

### Air Storage Cells

* Tiers from **1k → 256m**; if filled and more air is inserted, the cell may be **ejected**, and in extreme cases could **damage the drive**.
* **Portable** variants can connect directly to PNC machines (**maximum 20 bar**).
* Supported Upgrades: **Safety Card, Vacuum Card, (Portable) Charging Card**.
* **1m–256m** tiers require the **MEGA Storage Cells** mod, or can be always enabled via config.

### Amadron Wireless Terminal & Processing Station

* The terminal is energy-powered; **Shift-right-click the station** to bind and place orders; **unlimited** items can be turned into patterns for AE autocrafting.
* The station **dispatches a drone request every 200 ticks**; its **top must be unobstructed**, otherwise drones won’t deliver and items may clog.

## New Integration Recipes

* Multiple **Amadron trades** (Emeralds ↔ Certus/Fluix/Meteorite, ME casings, Blank Pattern, etc.).

> See **GuideME** in-game for full details.

## Dependencies & Compatibility

* Requires **Applied Energistics 2** and **PneumaticCraft: Repressurized**.
* **1m–256m cells**: their recipes require **MEGA Storage Cells** to be installed.
* **JEI/EMI** is recommended for recipe viewing; consult **GuideME** for mechanics and usage.
