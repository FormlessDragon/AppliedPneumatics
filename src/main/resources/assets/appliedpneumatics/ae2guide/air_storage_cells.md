# Air Storage Cells

<Row>
    <ItemImage id="air_cell_1k" scale="4" />
</Row>

Air Storage Cells provide the ME network with the ability to store air. Each byte can hold 250 mL of air. When the cell is full and additional air is inserted, it will be ejected from the drive, and may even cause the drive to explode.

## Portable Storage Cells
Portable storage cells can be inserted into a drive like a normal air storage cell, but they can also function as an air tank with a maximum pressure of 20 bar, directly interacting with PneumaticCraft machines.

## Available Upgrade Cards
- [Security Card](upgrades.md): Protects your drives and storage cells; when full, automatically rejects further insertion.
- [Vacuum Card](upgrades.md): When full, excess inserted air is automatically destroyed. Protects your drives in the same way as the Security Card.
- [Charging Card](upgrades.md): For portable storage cells; consumes energy and the stored air to charge gas-consuming items in the player’s inventory.
